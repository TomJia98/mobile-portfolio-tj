TuneSplice

The first major project I undertook, TuneSplice.
It is a front end application made in partnership with 2 other people in my UWA class, Tahlia and Jackson
Tunesplice is game that dynamically changes the lyrics of current top chart songs.
TuneSplice then displays them to you with a timer for you to try and guess what the original song was.

Junglr

The second major team project i had undertaken at the time, as well as the first major full stack application, was Junglr

Also made in conjunction with 2 other people from my UWA course, Daniel and Yi-Tsun Chen

Junglr is a Monkey themed social media website, which encrypts all the users data to anyone who isnt signed up.
This encryption is learnable, so if someone spends enough time on this webiste, they can learn to speak monkey.
It also features live refresh using websockets, as well as reacts and comments on posts.
